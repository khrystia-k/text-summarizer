The Ukrainian forces claim hitting a Russian S-300 missile system inside Russian territory with Western-supplied weapons. US President Biden allowed limited strikes in Russian territory around Kharkiv. Ukraine can use shorter-range GMLRS missiles but not long-range ATACMS. Military analysts see the policy change as an additional defense measure for Ukraine. Russia faced Ukrainian strikes with Western weapons in Crimea and other regions. Ukrainian President Zelensky appreciates the US decision to permit strikes in Russian territory.
